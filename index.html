Portfolio

My Recent Projects

CareWave - Telemedicine Platform

CareWave is a revolutionary telemedicine platform designed to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients in underserved regions. With features like remote consultations, real-time health monitoring, and AI-powered diagnostics, CareWave empowers communities with accessible, high-quality healthcare. This platform has already transformed healthcare delivery for thousands, reducing wait times and improving patient outcomes.

NfaakoAI Mentor

NfaakoAI is an AI-powered mentorship platform that connects medical students and young professionals with experienced mentors worldwide. Leveraging natural language processing and personalized learning algorithms, NfaakoAI provides tailored guidance, career advice, and educational resources. This platform is reshaping mentorship in healthcare, making it accessible, scalable, and impactful.

Agrisonic - Precision Agriculture Tool

Agrisonic is an innovative precision agriculture tool that uses ultrasonic sensors and AI to optimize crop yields and reduce resource waste. By providing real-time soil and crop health data, Agrisonic empowers farmers to make data-driven decisions, increasing productivity and sustainability. This project has the potential to revolutionize farming practices in developing regions.

DDPro - AI-Powered Drug Interaction Checker

DDPro is a cutting-edge AI tool that helps healthcare professionals and patients identify potential drug interactions in real time. With a comprehensive database and advanced algorithms, DDPro ensures safer medication management, reducing the risk of adverse drug reactions. This tool is already being used in clinics and pharmacies across Uganda, saving lives and improving patient care.

Ssuubi - AI Mental Health Chatbot

Ssuubi is an AI-powered mental health chatbot designed to provide immediate, confidential support to individuals struggling with mental health issues. Using natural language processing, Ssuubi offers personalized coping strategies, connects users with resources, and tracks emotional well-being over time. This project is breaking barriers in mental health care, making support accessible to all.

AskOsler - AI Medical Mentor

AskOsler is an AI-powered platform that provides medical students and healthcare professionals with instant access to medical knowledge, mentorship, and case-based learning. Named after the father of modern medicine, William Osler, this platform combines AI with curated medical content to empower the next generation of healthcare leaders.

CheckerPro - AI-Powered Symptom Checker

CheckerPro is an advanced AI tool that helps users identify potential health conditions based on symptoms. With a user-friendly interface and a robust medical database, CheckerPro provides accurate, actionable insights, empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their health. This tool is bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers.

SolverPro - Graphing Calculator with Desmos API

SolverPro is a powerful graphing calculator built using the Desmos API, designed to simplify complex mathematical computations for students and professionals. With features like real-time graphing, equation solving, and data visualization, SolverPro is revolutionizing the way people interact with mathematics.

Frank Shoe World - E-Commerce Platform

Frank Shoe World is a dynamic e-commerce platform for a Ugandan shoe retail business. Designed with a sleek, user-friendly interface, this platform offers secure payment options, personalized recommendations, and seamless order tracking. It’s transforming the way customers shop for footwear in Uganda.

Clinical Trials - Global Access App

This mobile app provides users with instant access to thousands of clinical trials worldwide. With advanced search filters, real-time updates, and personalized recommendations, the app simplifies the process of finding and participating in clinical trials, advancing medical research and patient care.

Mpox SIR Model - Disease Spread Simulation

The Mpox SIR Model is a sophisticated epidemiological tool that simulates the spread of infectious diseases like mpox. Using the Susceptible-Infected-Recovered (SIR) framework, this model helps public health officials predict outbreaks, allocate resources, and implement effective containment strategies.

Tubesnap - YouTube Video Downloader

Tubesnap is a versatile web tool that allows users to download YouTube videos effortlessly. With a simple, intuitive interface, Tubesnap supports multiple formats and resolutions, making it a favorite among content creators and educators.

EmrPro - Advanced Electronic Medical Records System

EmrPro is a state-of-the-art electronic medical records (EMR) system designed to streamline healthcare workflows. With features like real-time data synchronization, AI-powered analytics, and patient history tracking, EmrPro is transforming healthcare delivery in clinics and hospitals.

Clinical Text Analysis App

This innovative app uses natural language processing to analyze clinical text data, helping doctors track patient sentiment and improve care. By identifying patterns in patient feedback, this tool enhances communication and fosters better patient-doctor relationships.

HxU - Comprehensive Patient History Platform

HxU is an advanced platform that helps medical professionals take and store comprehensive patient histories. It also serves as a training tool for medical students, teaching them how to conduct thorough patient interviews. This platform is improving clinical efficiency and education in healthcare.

Enkuluze - Luganda Language Dictionary

Enkuluze is a comprehensive dictionary of Luganda, preserving and promoting one of Uganda’s most widely spoken languages. With thousands of entries, audio pronunciations, and cultural notes, Enkuluze is a vital resource for learners and speakers of Luganda.
